# What is the Performance Center?

The Performance Center is a telemetry-driven system that measures how AI impacts engineering performance.

## What it tracks

- productivity
- velocity
- AI usage patterns
- quality signals

## Key metrics

- AI Adoption
- AI Contribution Ratio
- Output per engineer
- Velocity acceleration

## What it enables

- objective performance insights
- coaching opportunities
- optimization of AI usage

## Outcome

Clear, measurable proof of AI-driven performance improvements.
